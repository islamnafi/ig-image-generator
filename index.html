The Prophet (sallallaahu ‘alayhi wa sallam) was asked,
"'Which of the women are best?' he replied, 'The one who pleases him when he looks at her, obeys him when he orders her and does not oppose him regarding herself or his wealth (and property) with what he dislikes.'"
Collected by Imaam Ahmad (2/251) and Imaam an-Nasaa’ee, “The Book of Marriage,” #3231
